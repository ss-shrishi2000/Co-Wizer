Do's And Dont's

Wash Your Hands

Frequently clean your hands with soap and water, or an alcohol-based hand rub. If you can, carry alcohol-based rub with you and use it often.

Last updated 3 mins ago

Wear Masks

Wear a well-fitting three-layer mask, especially when you can’t physically distance, or if you’re indoors. Clean your hands before putting on and taking off a mask.

Last updated 3 mins ago

Medical Attention

When it’s your turn, get vaccinated. Follow local guidance and recommendations about vaccination. Seek Medical care whenever needed.

Last updated 3 mins ago

Eat Healthy

Eating real food leads to eating more nutrient-rich food without much effort. Staying healthy shall always come from the nutrition u intake.

Last updated 3 mins ago

Social Distancing

Stay at least 1 metre away from others, even if they don’t appear to be sick. As the virus is highly contagious, try maintaining proper distance.

Last updated 3 mins ago

Disinfect

Make use of proper disinfectants so as to kill the existing germs on a given surface. Try cleaning and disinfecting every now and then to prevent the virus spread.
